About FITI

Bhutan's Financial Training Body

Supporting a stronger, more skilled financial sector for Bhutan's growing economy.

Our Mission

Building capacity in Bhutan's financial sector

FITI — the Financial Institutions Training Institute — was established to support Bhutan's financial sector by delivering practical, professional, and regulator-aligned training for banking, insurance, and finance professionals across the country.

The digital pilot represents FITI's move beyond traditional HR-based bulk enrolments into a more accessible and sustainable model — making learning available to individual professionals, institutions, and development partners alike.

The Digital Pilot

This MVP uses a deliberately low-infrastructure model powered by Google Classroom and Zoom. This approach allows FITI to test online learning without building an expensive new platform — keeping costs minimal while reaching more learners.

The pilot is designed to be self-funding from day one, with a total annual platform cost of just BTN 16,729 — covered by fewer than 15 individual course enrollments.

RMA Endorsement

All FITI courses are aligned with Royal Monetary Authority (RMA) guidelines and regulatory expectations. FITI certificates are recognised by RMA-regulated institutions across Bhutan's banking and insurance sectors.
