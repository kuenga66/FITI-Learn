Process

How It Works

From registration to certification — a simple four-step journey to professional upskilling.

1

Register your account

Sign up individually as a professional, or have your HR team register your institution for bulk enrolment.

2

Enroll in a course

Browse the catalogue, pay per course for individual access, or subscribe through an institutional plan.

3

Attend and learn

Join live Zoom sessions, access all course materials through Google Classroom, and revisit recordings anytime.

4

Get certified

Complete the online assessment and receive your FITI-issued digital certificate to share with your employer or on LinkedIn.

Platform Tools

Powered by tools you already use

No downloads or new accounts needed — FITI's pilot runs entirely on Google Classroom and Zoom.

📹

Zoom Live Sessions

All courses are delivered as interactive Zoom webinars with real-time Q&A, polls, and expert facilitators.

📚

Google Classroom

Course materials, assignments, case studies, and recordings are all organized in Google Classroom for easy access.

🎓

Digital Certificates

FITI-issued certificates are delivered digitally and can be verified by employers and regulators on request.
